Transforming SC Majority Villages into Adarsh Grams

A smart platform that uses data integration, AI, and GIS mapping to identify gaps, track projects, and prioritize interventions for declaring Adarsh Grams.

Key Features

GramSetu provides comprehensive solutions for village development tracking and management

Village Digital Twin (GIS Mapping)

Use satellite and GIS mapping to plot all village assets and identify gaps in services.

Real-Time Progress Tracking

Mobile app for officials to upload updates with AI verification of project completion.

AI-Powered Gap Analysis

AI compares village needs vs. existing services to generate priority scores for interventions.

Community Feedback Loop

Villagers can report missing amenities via IVR, WhatsApp bot, or local kiosk.

Dashboard for Ministry & Districts

Central dashboard to monitor all SC-majority villages and their development status.

Notification Alerts

Get notified about new government schemes and policies relevant to your village.
